# ADR-0304: Department Bounded Context

**Status:** Proposed

**Date:** 2026-07-16

**Deciders:** Architecture Board

---

## Context

Departments (ICU, Emergency, Radiology, Biomedical Engineering) are the organizational units of a hospital. They cut across the physical hierarchy вЂ” a patient in Room 302 can be in the ICU department. Departments cross-reference with Staffing Context (staff assignments) and Capacity Context (unit assignments).

This implements ADR-0052 (Department Hierarchy Model).

**Invariants:**
- A Department must belong to exactly one Organization
- A Department can have a parent Department (hierarchy depth в‰¤ 3 levels)
- A Unit must belong to exactly one Department
- A DepartmentAssignment must have exactly one Staff and one Department
- `end_date` must be after `start_date` if set
- Department head must be active Staff

**Domain Events:**
- `DepartmentCreated`, `DepartmentUpdated`, `DepartmentClosed`, `DepartmentHeadChanged`
- `UnitCreated`, `UnitClosed`, `StaffAssignedToDepartment`, `StaffRemovedFromDepartment`
- `DepartmentGroupCreated`

---

## Consequences

**Positive:**
- Department hierarchy enables reporting by organizational unit
- Department assignments link Staffing (staff) to organizational structure
- Independent of physical hierarchy (Room в†” Department relationship via assignments)

**Negative:**
- Staff can have multiple department assignments (complex queries)
- Department restructuring requires reassignment cascade
