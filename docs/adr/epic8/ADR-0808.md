# ADR-0808: Alerting Strategy

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-20

**Deciders:** Architecture Board

---

## Context

Effective alerting ensures critical issues are caught and addressed promptly.

## Decision

We will implement **Hierarchical Alerting**:

### Alert Levels

| Level | Severity | Response Time | Channel |
|-------|----------|---------------|---------|
| P1 | Critical | 15 min | SMS + Call |
| P2 | High | 1 hour | SMS |
| P3 | Medium | 4 hours | Email |
| P4 | Low | 24 hours | Slack |

### Alert Rules

```yaml
alerts:
  - name: "ServiceDown"
    severity: P1
    condition: "up == 0"
    duration: "1m"
  - name: "HighErrorRate"
    severity: P2
    condition: "error_rate > 5"
    duration: "5m"
  - name: "SlowResponse"
    severity: P3
    condition: "p95_latency > 1000"
    duration: "10m"
  - name: "DiskSpaceLow"
    severity: P4
    condition: "disk_free < 10"
    duration: "30m"
```

### On-Call Rotation

- Primary: Engineer on-call
- Secondary: Team lead
- Escalation: Engineering manager
