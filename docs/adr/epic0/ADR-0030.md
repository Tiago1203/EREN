# ADR-0030: Kubernetes Deployment

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-05-01

**Deciders:** Infrastructure

---

## Context

EREN needs a container orchestration platform for deployment, scaling, and management of services across environments.

---

## Decision

**Kubernetes is the deployment platform.** (Superseded by ADR-0080 for detailed implementation.)

---

## Summary

- Production: Amazon EKS or Google GKE (managed Kubernetes)
- Staging: Self-managed Kubernetes on VMs
- Development: Docker Compose
