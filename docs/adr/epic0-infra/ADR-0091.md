# ADR-0091: Service Mesh (Istio vs Linkerd)

**Status:** Proposed

**Date:** 2026-07-16

**Deciders:** Infrastructure Team

---

## Context

EREN may benefit from a service mesh for mTLS between services, traffic management, and observability within the cluster.

---

## Decision

**PENDING — Not required for v1.**

A service mesh (Istio or Linkerd) adds operational complexity. It is **NOT required for v1** because:
- mTLS is not required between internal services (single-tenant, internal network)
- Kubernetes network policies provide sufficient isolation
- Ingress controller (NGINX) handles external TLS

**This ADR is PROPOSED for future consideration (v2+).**

---

## Istio vs Linkerd

| Feature | Istio | Linkerd |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Complexity | High | Low |
| mTLS | Yes | Yes |
| Traffic management | Advanced | Basic |
| Observability | Built-in | Built-in |
| Performance overhead | ~10-15% | ~1-2% |
