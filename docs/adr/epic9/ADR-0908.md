# ADR-0908: Dataset Management

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-20

**Deciders:** Architecture Board

---

## Context

Gestión de datasets para training y evaluation.

---

## Decision

Dataset Management con:

1. **Data Sources**
   - Historical interactions
   - User feedback
   - Medical guidelines
   - De-identified patient data

2. **Processing Pipeline**
   - Data validation
   - Cleaning & normalization
   - Augmentation
   - Quality checks

3. **Storage**
   - Versioned datasets
   - Feature store
   - Backup & recovery

---

## Consequences

**Pros:**
- Reproducibility
- Data quality
- Efficient training

**Cons:**
- Privacy compliance
- Storage costs
- Pipeline maintenance
