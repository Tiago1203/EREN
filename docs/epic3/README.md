# EREN Epic 3 — Hospital Management Platform

*Version 1.1 - 2026-07-20*

Epic 3 implements the hospital management domain with Capacity, Staffing, Organization, Department, Asset Management, and Inventory bounded contexts.

---

## Components

### Capacity Management
- Bed capacity tracking
- Utilization metrics

### Staffing Management
- Staff scheduling
- Skills matrix

### Organization
- Multi-campus support
- Organizational hierarchy

### Department
- Department hierarchy
- Resource allocation

### Asset Management
- Hospital equipment tracking
- Location management

### Inventory
- Medical supplies tracking
- Reorder levels
