# ADR-1006: Support SLA Definitions

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-20

**Deciders:** Architecture Board

---

## Context

SLAs para soporte empresarial.

---

## Decision

Support tiers:

| Tier | Response Time | Resolution | Support Channel |
|------|--------------|------------|-----------------|
| Community | Best effort | Community | Forum |
| Professional | 24h | Business days | Email |
| Enterprise | 4h | 24/7 | Phone/Email |

---

## Consequences

**Pros:**
- Clear expectations
- Tiered pricing
- Customer satisfaction

**Cons:**
- Resource allocation
- Escalation process
