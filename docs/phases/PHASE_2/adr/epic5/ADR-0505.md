# ADR-0505: Failure Prediction Model

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-20

**Deciders:** Architecture Board

---

## Context

Proactive maintenance prevents device failures and improves uptime. The Failure Prediction model identifies devices at risk.

## Decision

We will implement a **predictive maintenance system**:

### Risk Indicators
- Age of device
- Usage hours
- Error frequency
- Performance metrics
- Environmental conditions

### Risk Scoring
```
Risk Score = Σ(indicator_weight × indicator_value) / Σ(indicator_weight)
```

### Risk Categories

| Score | Category | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| 80-100 | Critical | Immediate service |
| 60-79 | High | Schedule within 7 days |
| 40-59 | Medium | Schedule within 30 days |
| 0-39 | Low | Routine maintenance |

## Consequences

### Positive
- Reduced unplanned downtime
- Optimized maintenance resources
- Extended equipment life

### Negative
- Requires historical data
- Model accuracy depends on data quality
