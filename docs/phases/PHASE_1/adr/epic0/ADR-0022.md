# ADR-0022: Trust as Cross-Cutting Service

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-04-15

**Deciders:** Clinical Team

---

## Context

Trust is a cross-cutting concern. Every CDS response must pass through a trust assessment layer before being delivered to the user. This assessment affects confidence scores, response formatting, and escalation decisions.

---

## Decision

**Trust is a cross-cutting cognitive capability that wraps every CDS response.**

The Trust capability evaluates each response for:
- Evidence quality
- Consistency with prior knowledge
- Uncertainty quantification
- Potential biases
- Source reliability

---

## Trust Thresholds

| Trust Level | Confidence Range | Response Format |
|------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| HIGH | 0.8-1.0 | Standard recommendation with evidence |
| MEDIUM | 0.5-0.79 | Recommendation with uncertainty notes |
| LOW | 0.0-0.49 | Suggestion with strong caveats |
