# ADR-0051: Organization Multi-Campus Strategy

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-16

**Deciders:** Architecture Board

---

## Context

Hospitals often operate multiple campuses. Each campus has its own buildings, departments, and staff. EREN must support multi-campus organizations.

---

## Decision

**All campuses share the same tenant_id but have distinct campus_id, building_id, and floor_id.**

Multi-tenancy is enforced at the tenant level. Within a tenant, campuses are distinguished by `campus_id` in all location entities.

---

## Campus Data Model

| Field | Type | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| tenant_id | UUID | Multi-tenant isolation |
| campus_id | UUID | Campus identifier |
| building_id | UUID | Building within campus |
| floor_id | UUID | Floor within building |
| room_id | UUID | Room within floor |

---

## Cross-Campus Queries

All queries include `tenant_id` AND `campus_id` for proper scoping.
