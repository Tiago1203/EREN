# ADR-0020: Clinical Decision Support as Supporting Domain

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-04-01

**Deciders:** Clinical Team

---

## Context

Clinical Decision Support (CDS) is a key capability of EREN. It must be integrated into the Clinical domain but as a supporting service — it advises, never decides.

---

## Decision

**Clinical Decision Support is a Supporting Domain (as defined in Domain-Driven Design).**

CDS depends on the Clinical domain (Patient, Diagnosis, Treatment contexts) but does not own patient data. It provides recommendations that clinicians can accept or reject.

---

## Bounded Context

CDS has its own bounded context (`Clinical Decision Support Context`) but receives data from:
- Patient Context (symptoms, history)
- Diagnosis Context (ICD codes)
- Device Context (device data, alarms)
- Biomedical Context (maintenance history)

CDS publishes recommendations back to the Clinical domain.

---

## Human Authority Principle

CDS recommendations are **advisory only**. EREN never makes clinical decisions on behalf of healthcare providers.
