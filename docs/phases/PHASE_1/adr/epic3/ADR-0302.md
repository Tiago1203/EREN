# ADR-0302: Staffing Bounded Context

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-16

**Deciders:** Architecture Board

---

## Context

The Staffing Context manages hospital personnel: staff, roles, teams, shifts, and schedules. It tracks who works where, when, and with what qualifications. Staff assignments link to Capacity Context (unit assignments) and Treatment Context (ordering clinicians).

---

## Decision

**Aggregate: Staff** (aggregate root)

```
Staff (aggregate root)
├── tenant_id, staff_id
├── employee_id: string (hospital employee number)
├── first_name, last_name
├── email, phone
├── staff_type: enum (physician, nurse, technician, engineer, admin, other)
├── employment_status: enum (active, on_leave, terminated)
├── hire_date: date
├── qualifications: list[Qualification] (value object)
└── certifications: list[Certification] (value object)

Role
├── tenant_id, role_id
├── role_name: string (e.g., "ICU Nurse", "Biomedical Engineer")
├── role_type: enum (clinical, technical, administrative)
├── permissions: list[Permission]
└── qualifications_required: list[Qualification]

Team
├── tenant_id, team_id
├── team_name: string
├── team_type: enum (nursing, biomedical, engineering, administrative)
├── department_id (FK)
└── team_lead_staff_id (FK)

Shift
├── tenant_id, shift_id, staff_id (FK)
├── shift_type: enum (day, evening, night, on_call)
├── start_time: datetime
├── end_time: datetime
├── unit_id (FK — capacity unit for assignment)
├── status: enum (scheduled, in_progress, completed, cancelled)
└── notes: string (optional)
```

**Invariants:**
- A shift must have exactly one assigned staff member
- A staff member cannot have overlapping shifts
- Shift assignments must respect qualification requirements
- Team lead must be an active staff member
- Staff with `employment_status = terminated` cannot be assigned new shifts

**Domain Events:**
- `StaffRegistered`, `StaffUpdated`, `StaffTerminated`
- `RoleAssigned`, `RoleRemoved`, `QualificationAdded`
- `TeamCreated`, `StaffAssignedToTeam`, `StaffRemovedFromTeam`
- `ShiftScheduled`, `ShiftStarted`, `ShiftEnded`, `ShiftCancelled`

---

## Consequences

**Positive:**
- Staff is aggregate root — employment lifecycle centered here
- Role-based qualifications enforced at domain level
- Shift scheduling respects availability and qualifications
- Links to Capacity (unit) and Department contexts

**Negative:**
- Complex shift scheduling algorithm needed
- Qualification expiry tracking requires background job
