# ADR-0506: Risk Assessment Framework

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-20

**Deciders:** Architecture Board

---

## Context

Medical devices carry inherent risks. The Risk Assessment Framework evaluates and quantifies risks to prioritize mitigation efforts.

## Decision

We will implement a **risk-based prioritization system** based on ISO 14971:

### Risk Matrix

| Likelihood | Severity | Risk Level |
|------------|----------|------------|
| Frequent | Catastrophic | Critical |
| Occasional | Major | High |
| Remote | Moderate | Medium |
| improbable | Negligible | Low |

### Risk Score Formula
```
Risk Score = Likelihood × Severity × Detectability
```

### Risk Categories

| Score | Category | Response Time |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| 20-25 | Critical | 24 hours |
| 12-19 | High | 7 days |
| 6-11 | Medium | 30 days |
| 1-5 | Low | 90 days |

## Consequences

### Positive
- Systematic risk management
- Compliance with regulations
- Prioritized actions

### Negative
- Requires accurate data
- May be perceived as bureaucratic
