# ADR-0503: Differential Diagnosis

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-20

**Deciders:** Architecture Board

---

## Context

When engineers encounter symptoms or error codes, they need help identifying the most likely causes. The Differential Diagnosis engine matches symptoms against known patterns.

## Decision

We will implement a **symptom-to-cause matching system**:

### 1. Symptom Collection
- Error codes
- Warning messages
- Behavioral observations
- Environmental factors

### 2. Pattern Matching
- Known device profiles
- Historical incidents
- Manufacturer documentation

### 3. Probability Scoring
```
P(cause | symptoms) = P(symptoms | cause) × P(cause) / P(symptoms)
```

### Output

| Rank | Possible Cause | Probability | Confidence |
|------|---------------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Power supply failure | 85% | High |
| 2 | Firmware corruption | 60% | Medium |
| 3 | Sensor malfunction | 25% | Low |

## Consequences

### Positive
- Faster diagnosis
- Reduced mean time to repair
- Comprehensive cause consideration

### Negative
- Requires extensive knowledge base
- May miss rare causes
