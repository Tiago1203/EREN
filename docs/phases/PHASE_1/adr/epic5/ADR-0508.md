# ADR-0508: Compliance Advisor

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-20

**Deciders:** Architecture Board

---

## Context

Medical devices are subject to extensive regulations. The Compliance Advisor ensures devices meet all applicable requirements.

## Decision

We will implement a **compliance monitoring system**:

### Regulatory Frameworks
- FDA (21 CFR Part 820)
- EU MDR (2017/745)
- ISO 13485
- AAMI standards

### Compliance Checks
- Pre-purchase validation
- Installation verification
- Periodic audits
- Incident reporting

### Documentation Requirements
- Device history records
- Maintenance logs
- Calibration certificates
- Risk assessments

## Consequences

### Positive
- Avoid regulatory penalties
- Patient safety assurance
- Audit readiness

### Negative
- Administrative overhead
- Complex requirements
