# ADR-0903: Fine Tuning Strategy

**Status:** Accepted

**Date:** 2026-07-20

**Deciders:** Architecture Board

---

## Context

Necesitamos ajustar modelos base con datos específicos del dominio hospitalario.

---

## Decision

Estrategia de Fine Tuning con:

1. **LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation)**
   - Eficiente en parámetros
   - Rápido entrenamiento
   - Bajo costo

2. **RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback)**
   - Reward model training
   - PPO optimization
   - Quality alignment

3. **Domain Adaptation**
   - Medical terminology
   - Hospital workflows
   - Clinical guidelines

---

## Consequences

**Pros:**
- Modelos especializados
- Cost-effective
- Rápida iteración

**Cons:**
- Requiere GPUs para training
- Expertise en ML
